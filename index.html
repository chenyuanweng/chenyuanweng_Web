貓貓工作室

類別:工作室理念

藝術家的工作室除了創作的氣息外，還有著他們平日生活的軌跡。那裡同時出現作品、創作工具，以及各類私人物品，使得公共展示空間與個人私密空間的界線逐漸模糊，多了隨興的日常氛圍。參觀者還可以在這些工作室與創作者親身對話，感受直接又充滿親切的藝術交流。
藝術工作室所呈現的景色，也不同於美術館或藝廊中的展覽。因為展覽通常是經由策展團隊的精心安排，才呈現在世人眼前，但在藝術家的工作室裡，已完成的作品、進行中的作品、新想法的初稿、牆面或黑板上的計劃進程，都在在散發著藝術孕育與茁壯的過程。藝術在這裡是處於進行中、隨時充滿變動的狀態。
In addition to the atmosphere of creation, artists' studios also contain the traces of their daily lives. Works, creative tools, and various personal items appear there at the same time, making the boundaries between public exhibition space and personal private space gradually blurred, adding a casual daily atmosphere. Visitors can also have personal conversations with creators in these studios and experience direct and cordial artistic communication.
The scenery presented in an art studio is also different from an exhibition in an art museum or gallery. Because exhibitions are usually carefully arranged by the curatorial team before they are presented to the world, but in the artist's studio, completed works, works in progress, first drafts of new ideas, and the planning process on the wall or blackboard, They are all exuding the process of art nurturing and growing. Art here is in a state of progress and full of changes at any time.
